The Internet is Everything, 2019

Moross does not use several different colors in this artwork, but this does not bother the observer's eye, because the use of the seven colors present in the poster is adjusted through the use of white paint, which is used to add details to the background design, but what makes the use of white much more important is that it is used to represent the poster subject. The background is very imaginary, so as to highlight the message that each of us have the answer at hand but it is overshadowed by our necessity to use the Internet to solve all our problems.
