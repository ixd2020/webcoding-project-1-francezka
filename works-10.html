Wired "Talent Tube", 2013
Typographic Comission for Wired Magazine Feature on YouTube Talent


Moross agreed to collab with Wired Magazine to create a typographic comission for the YouTube Talent feature. By incorporating geometric shapes with the typeface, Moross creates a unique 3D design that fits perfectly well with Wired's eccentric style.
