Dream Come True, 2016
In collaboration with Jenue and Simba Sleep


To build a limited edition set of designer mattresses, Moross collaborated with 3D artist, Jenue and Simba Sleep, a design-led mattress company. Every purchase will help the charity deliver and contribute to the Christmas Crisis, which provides more than 11,000 homeless guests with hot food, accommodation and companionship during the festive season each year. In Moross and Jenue's 'Dreams Come True' matress, they reveals their love of typography and color, alluding playfully to all the positive things that can be happen in bed. Moross had in mind, from the beginning, to make a 3D original and colourful type. Both artists combined their skills in 3D and 2D design in order to achieve a different and unique style.
