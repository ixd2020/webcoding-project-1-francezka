Kate Moross X Keith Harring, 2020
Portrait Series for Infringe Magazine


Moross recently agreed to collaborate with Adobe to create a new Adobe x Keith Haring digital brush set. As the final outcome of the project is a digital paiting, they chose this specific quote as they felt that it really worked with the whole concept of the whole project. The quote also struck a chord with Moross as they felt that making and creating work does not need a defined purpose, but rather as a form of self-expression.
