Spice World Art Direction, 2019
Branding and Art Direction for Spice World 2019 Uk & Ireland Tour.


Moross produced the art direction and live visuals design for Spice World 2019 UK & Ireland tour. As part of a core creative team led by Creative Director Lee Lodge, they partnered with Studio Moross to facilitate the art direction and design across the screens and stage. Together, they created a new typeface that referenced past iterations of the iconic Spice Girls logos while bringing it into 2019 as well. The font was also designed to perform in relation to the early use of SPICE font, both squashed and extended. This was done in order to capture the style of design in the 90s but also with a new take.
