Cult Hair, Reworked, 2017
Portrait Series for Infringe Magazine


Kate Moross is an illustrator, art director, director and designer based in London. Known for their bold, vivid aesthetics, Moross varied practice includes murals, magazine covers, music videos and textiles. They designed pieces for Adidas, Topshop and Converse. For Cult Hair Reworked, Moross introduces their trademark use of vibrant color and stylized graphic squiggles.
