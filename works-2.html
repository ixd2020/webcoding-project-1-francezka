Princess Principles, Disney 2016


Disney has partnered with parents, illustrators and research experts "to encourage young girls to follow their dreams," redefining the qualities that a modern day princess should embody.

In communicating with 5,000 parents about what qualities their daughters will like to have, Disney has created The Princess Principles, ten points that include caring for others, loyalty, honesty, trust, and to ultimately try your best. As a result of its research it is “clear that being a ‘princess’ is not just about titles or tiaras or marrying a prince, but about emulating Cinderella’s courage, Merida’s heroism and Snow White’s generosity.”

The final principles are visualised through typographic based posters from three of UK's leading illustrators. The different posters are available free of charge through Disney in-store and online.
