Kate is an Art Director, Illustrator and Graphic Designer

Kate's work includes artistic direction, moving imagery, typography and illustration. In 2012, Kate formed Studio Moross, a multidisciplinary design company based in London, as an extension of her own work, and a means of collaborating with other creative people.
